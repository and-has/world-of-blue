How do I print my own cyanotype at home?

  1. First, Prepare the Solution:
    • Mix equal parts Ferric Ammonium Citrate and Potassium Ferricyanide with water in separate containers (e.g., 1 teaspoon each).
    • Combine the two solutions in equal parts to create the sensitizer. Stir gently.
  2. Coat the Paper:
    • In a dark or dimly lit room, use a brush or sponge to evenly coat the paper or fabric with the sensitizer.
    • Allow the coated paper to dry completely in the dark.
  3. Expose the Print:
    • Place objects or a transparency film on the dried, sensitized paper.
    • Secure with a glass or acrylic sheet to keep the design flat.
    • Expose to sunlight or a UV lamp for 10-20 minutes until the paper turns a bronze color.
  4. Develop the Print:
    • Rinse the exposed paper in running water for about 5 minutes until the water runs clear.
